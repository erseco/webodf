Editing and Applying Paragraph Styles in Documents

Next to direct styling, there are also defined styles which can be assigned. WebODF already supports setting defined styles to paragraphs. And it supports the creation of new paragraph style definition and the modification or deletion of existing ones.

Try editing and applying paragraph styles. Use the paragraph style selector in the toolbar to assign existing styles, then open the paragraph styles dialog to create a new style, modify its formatting, or remove an existing definition.

Tip: click inside a paragraph, then use the style dropdown in the toolbar to switch between named paragraph styles. The demo loads a sample document automatically.
